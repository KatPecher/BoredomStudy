# Codebook BoredomStudy
Author: Katja Pecher

SPO-14560 Project Seminar I, University Konstanz, SoSe 2026

---

**Limesurvey** - Questionnaire data that participants filled out before, during and after the balance test. Includes Subject IDs, height, weight, state and trait boredom, ratings of effort, boredom and pain during the exercise.

ls_raw - unedited limesurvey export

ls_proc - processed limesurvey data

lime - limesurvey data as used in the analysis, identical to ls_proc but more readable variable name

---
**Balance** - Sway data measured during the trials, tracking the participant at back, hips, wrists and the VR goggles.

balance_raw - unedited anorepia export

balance_proc - processed balance data

balance - balance data as used in the analysis, identical to balance_proc but more readable variable name

---
**PsychoPy** - Random movements manually tracked during the trials, categories are hands, head, legs and other. 

psychopy_raw - unedited psychopy export

psychopy_proc - processed psychopy data

psychopy - random movement data as used in the analysis, identical to psychopy_proc but more readable variable name
